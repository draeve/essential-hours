More Information on COVID-19:

COVID-19 has taken over 88,000 lives and infected almost 1.5 million people in the United States.



To help those around us, it is important to do our part in slowing the spread of the virus. Remember to:

  • wash your hands
  • have good hygiene
  • wear masks in public at all times

Some common symptoms include:

  • shortness of breath
  • sore throat
  • fever

If you experience the above symptoms, be sure to call your local healthcare provider to find out if you should be tested.
